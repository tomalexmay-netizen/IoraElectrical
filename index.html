About Our Residential Services

Your Home, Our Priority.

At Iora Electrical, we believe every family deserves a home that is safe, efficient, and brilliantly lit. We understand that home electrical issues can be stressful, which is why we’ve built our residential service around honesty, transparency, and affordability.

Whether you’re looking to upgrade to a smart home, need a complete rewire, or just have a flickering light, our team treats your house with respect. No hidden fees—just clear communication and expert craftsmanship.

Typical residential work includes:

  • Consumer unit upgrades and safety checks.
  • Full and partial rewires.
  • Indoor and outdoor lighting design.
  • EV charger and smart home installations.
  • Fault finding and remedial work.
